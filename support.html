FAQ

What is Euphoriax?
Euphoriax is a company specializing in selling verse codes and map templates for the UEFN (Unreal Editor for Fortnite).

How does Euphoriax collaborate with others?
We collaborate with individuals or groups who contribute to the creation of verse codes and map templates. These collaborators receive a share of the profits generated from the sale of their contributions.

Where can I purchase products from Euphoriax?
You can purchase our products from our Patreon website or through authorized distributors.

Are there any licensing restrictions on the products?
Our products are for personal use only. They cannot be sold or distributed commercially without obtaining proper licensing from Euphoriax. Unauthorized resale or distribution is strictly prohibited.

Can I customize the verse codes and map templates I purchase?
Yes, our products are designed to be easily customizable to suit your project's needs.

Does Euphoriax provide support for its products?
Yes, we offer customer support to assist with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our products. Please note that personalized assistance is prioritized for customers with a dedicated support role. However, we are also happy to assist you whenever we have available time.

How can I become a collaborator with Euphoriax?
If you're interested in collaborating with us, please reach out to our team through the contact information provided on our website. We're always open to discussing potential partnerships.

Is Euphoriax involved in any community initiatives or events?
Yes, we actively participate in community initiatives, events, and forums related to the UEFN development. Keep an eye on our website and social media channels for updates on our involvement. Don't forget to join our Discord community and follow us on social media for more updates and interactions!

Where can I find updates or news about Euphoriax?
You can stay updated on the latest news, product releases, and announcements from Euphoriax by following us on our website and social media platforms.

How can I contact Euphoriax for further inquiries?
For any additional questions or inquiries, please feel free to contact us through the email or phone number provided on our website's contact page. We'll be happy to assist you.

PAYMENT OPTIONS

Payment Methods
We utilize Patreon as our primary payment method, offering convenience and flexibility for our customers. You can easily select Patreon as your preferred payment option during the checkout process.

Security and Privacy
Your payment information is treated with the highest standards of security and privacy. Patreon employs encryption technology to safeguard your data, ensuring that your transactions are secure and confidential.

Payment Confirmation
Once your payment is successfully processed through Patreon, you will receive an email confirmation. This email will provide a detailed summary of your order and payment details for your records.
